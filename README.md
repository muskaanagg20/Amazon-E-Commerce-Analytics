# 🛒 Amazon E-Commerce Analytics — Business Case Study

## 📌 Context
Simulated a real-world e-commerce scenario where the business lacked clarity on:
- Revenue drivers
- Customer value segmentation
- Return behavior
- Delivery inefficiencies
- Underperforming products

---

## ❗ Problem Statements
The objective was to answer key business questions:

- Why are certain months underperforming in sales?
- Do product ratings actually influence revenue?
- What is driving high return rates?
- Which high-value customers should be retained?
- Why are some expensive products not selling well?
- How does delivery type impact customer experience?

---

## ⚙️ Solution Approach

### 1. Data Preparation
- Cleaned raw dataset (null handling, duplicates, type corrections)
- Structured relational model (Customers ↔ Orders)

### 2. Analytical Layer (SQL)
- Built revenue metrics, MoM growth, rolling averages
- Designed **customer scoring model** (Revenue + Frequency + AOV)
- Identified top customers and behavioral patterns

### 3. Visualization Layer (Power BI)
- Developed interactive dashboard to track:
  - Sales trends
  - Customer segments
  - Product performance
  - Returns & logistics

---

## 🔍 Key Findings

### 📉 Sales Performance
- February & June consistently underperformed  
→ Missed seasonal targeting opportunities  

### ⭐ Customer Behavior
- Strong correlation: **Higher ratings → Higher revenue**  
→ Trust directly impacts sales  

### 🔁 Returns Insight
- Majority returns due to **defective products**  
→ Direct impact on profitability  

### 💰 Product Strategy Gap
- High-priced items (e.g., cameras) had **low volume sales**  
→ Pricing ≠ demand  

### 🚚 Operational Bottleneck
- “Shipped from Abroad” orders had **highest delays**  
→ Poor delivery experience  

---

## 📊 Business Impact

- Enabled **customer segmentation strategy**:
  - Platinum / Gold → retention focus  
  - Silver / Bronze → re-engagement & upselling  

- Identified **revenue leakage due to returns**

- Highlighted **supply chain inefficiencies**

- Uncovered **hidden growth opportunities** in low-performing months

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

- 🎯 Launch targeted campaigns in low-sales months  
- 🛠️ Improve product quality to reduce returns  
- 📦 Optimize logistics for international shipping  
- 💬 Leverage high-rated products in marketing  
- 💰 Promote underperforming high-value products
